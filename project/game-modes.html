Story Mode

Players embark on a narrative-driven journey through a fictional world where they must solve coding challenges to progress. The story mode is divided into chapters, each focusing on different aspects of JavaScript, CSS, or HTML. As players advance, they encounter various characters who provide quests or challenges that require specific coding solutions.

Challenges:

-Level 1:Basic syntax and variables.

-Level 2:Functions and loops

Level 3:DOM manipulation and events.

-Level 4:Advanced topics like API calls or CSS animations.

-Rewards:Players earn experience points, unlock new abilities, and discover hidden areas of the map as they complete coding tasks

Time Attack Mode

-In this mode, players race against the clock to complete coding challenges. Each challenge has a strict time limit, and players must quickly apply their knowledge to solve problems before time runs out. The faster they solve the challenge, the more points they earn.

-Challenges:

-Basic Challenges:Quick tasks like fixing syntax errors or writing simple functions.

-Intermediate Challenges:More complex problems requiring loops, arrays, or object manipulation.

-Advanced Challenges:Tasks involving algorithm optimization, responsive design, or complex animations.

-Rewards:High scores, leaderboard rankings, and special badges for speed and accuracy.
